The Afrobeats Takeover

By 2024, Afrobeats was streamed more than twice as much outside Africa as within Africa, completing its transformation from a regional sound into a global movement.

How Afrobeats Streaming Grew Worldwide

Which Songs Drove Global Reach?

Songs that charted in the most countries helped introduce Afrobeats to new audiences worldwide.

While Afrobeats originated in African markets, the fastest growth came from audiences outside Africa. Songs such as Calm Down, Love Nwantiti, and Essence charted across dozens of countries and helped transform the genre into a global phenomenon

Key Insights

  • Calm Down charted in 58 countries, the highest of any song in the dataset.
  • Love Nwantiti generated 15.4 million TikTok videos, making it the most viral song in the dataset.
  • Streams in Africa grew from 669.5 million in 2019 to 2.67 billion in 2024.
  • Streams outside Africa grew from 1.39 billion in 2019 to 5.61 billion in 2024.
